Rods
- The rods (spark rod, ember rod, and frost rod) now use the same mechanic as the elemental guns(blaster, blaze and glacial guns).  They cast their respective spell instead of physically attacking a unit.

Bows & Guns
- Bows and Crossbows now have their range vary per item.
- The Stoneshooter no longer turns you into stone at the beginning of battle.

Jobs/Classes
- The Squire can now use daggers, crossbows, and swords
